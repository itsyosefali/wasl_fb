Privacy Policy

Wasl Meta (Meta Gateway)
Effective date: June 9, 2026
Website: https://meta.itsyosefali.cloud

This Privacy Policy describes how Wasl Meta (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you connect a Meta (Facebook) Page and use our Messenger inbox and related services.

By connecting your Facebook Page or using our application, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Who we are

Wasl Meta is a customer communication tool that lets businesses manage Facebook Messenger conversations through a unified inbox. The service is operated by itsyosefali and made available at meta.itsyosefali.cloud.

2. Information we collect

When you connect a Facebook Page and use our service, we may collect and process:

  • Page information — Page name, Page ID, and connection status.
  • Access tokens — Facebook Page access tokens obtained through Meta OAuth, stored in encrypted form, used only to send and receive messages on your behalf.
  • Messenger messages — Message content, timestamps, message IDs, and direction (incoming or outgoing).
  • Contact identifiers — Facebook Page-Scoped User IDs (PSIDs) and, when provided by Meta, contact names or profile information.
  • Technical data — API request logs, webhook delivery metadata, and connection events needed to operate and secure the service.

We do not collect your Facebook password. Authentication is handled entirely through Meta’s official OAuth flow.

3. How we use information

We use the information above solely to:

  • Display Messenger conversations in your inbox.
  • Send replies you compose through the application.
  • Receive and process webhooks from Meta when customers message your Page.
  • Maintain connection to your Page and keep the service secure and reliable.
  • Provide real-time updates to authorized users of your account.

We do not sell your data. We do not use Messenger data for advertising, profiling, or unrelated marketing purposes.

4. Legal basis and Meta Platform terms

Our use of Meta data is governed by the Meta Platform Terms and applicable Meta policies. We access Meta data only for the purposes disclosed in this policy and authorized by you when connecting your Page.

5. Data storage and security

  • Data is stored on secure servers operated by our hosting provider.
  • Page access tokens are encrypted at rest.
  • API access is protected by authentication keys and HTTPS in transit.
  • We retain data only as long as needed to provide the service or as required by law.

6. Data sharing

We may share data only in these limited cases:

  • Meta (Facebook) — when sending or receiving messages through the Graph API and webhooks, as required for the integration to function.
  • Service providers — hosting, database, or infrastructure providers that process data on our behalf under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements — when required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.

If you configure outbound webhooks to your own systems (for example, an ERP), message events may be forwarded to endpoints you control.

7. Data retention and deletion

We retain Messenger data while your Page remains connected and as needed for inbox history. You may request deletion of your data by disconnecting your Page and contacting us at the address below. We will delete or anonymize stored data within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you or your Page.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent by disconnecting your Facebook Page from our application.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details in Section 10. You may also manage permissions through your Facebook App Settings.

9. Children

Our service is intended for businesses and authorized Page administrators. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
